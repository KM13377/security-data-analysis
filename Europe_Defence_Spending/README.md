NATO Defence Spending Analysis (2014–2025)
Project Overview
This project analyzes defence expenditure as a percentage of GDP for Poland, Germany, and France between 2014 and 2025 using NATO data.

Research Question
How have defence spending patterns changed among key European NATO members after Russia’s actions in Ukraine and the 2022 full-scale invasion?

Key Findings
Poland increased defence spending from approximately 1.9% of GDP in 2014 to over 4.4% in 2025.
Germany increased spending significantly after 2022.
France maintained relatively stable spending levels above the NATO benchmark.
