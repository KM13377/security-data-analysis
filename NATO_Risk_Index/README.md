# NATO Risk Index

## Objective
Identify NATO countries facing the highest security pressure based on defence spending, geographic exposure and threat environment.

## Methodology

Risk Index = Defence Spending (% GDP) × Threat Level

Threat Level:
- 5 = Baltic States
- 4 = Poland, Finland
- 3 = Central Europe
- 2 = Western Europe

## Results

Top countries by Risk Index:

| Country | Risk Index |
|----------|----------|
| Poland | 16.4 |
| Estonia | 16.0 |
| Latvia | 14.5 |

## Key Findings

- Poland achieved the highest Risk Index score.
- Baltic countries dominate the ranking.
- Geographic proximity to Russia strongly influences defence posture.
- Higher military expenditure reflects increased perception of security threats.
